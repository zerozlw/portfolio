## Enterprise AI is Different

Consumer AI and enterprise AI are fundamentally different games. Here's what I've learned.

## Lesson 1: Trust Before Intelligence

Enterprises don't care how smart your AI is if they can't trust it. Start with:
- **Explainability** — Show your work
- **Auditability** — Log everything
- **Control** — Let administrators set boundaries

## Lesson 2: Integration > Innovation

A 70% accurate solution that integrates with existing workflows beats a 99% accurate standalone tool every time.

## Lesson 3: Data is the Real Challenge

The AI model is the easy part. The hard part is:
- Data quality and consistency
- Access control and privacy
- Real-time vs batch processing
- Legacy system integration

## The Playbook

1. **Find the pain** — What do engineers hate doing?
2. **Start small** — One workflow, one team
3. **Measure relentlessly** — Time saved, errors reduced, satisfaction
4. **Scale what works** — Don't try to boil the ocean

## Conclusion

Enterprise AI is a marathon, not a sprint. Build trust, prove value, then expand.
