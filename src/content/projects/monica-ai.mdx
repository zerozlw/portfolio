## Background

Monica is an AI browser extension targeting **overseas users**, bringing ChatGPT-level capabilities directly into the browser — chat, quick actions, reading assistant, ChatPDF, YouTube summary, and more. As the product grew more complex, a pattern emerged: **most users only scratched the surface**. They used Chat and Quick Actions, ignored everything else, and had no reason to upgrade to paid plans.

Meanwhile, user feedback was flowing in daily through email — feature requests, pain points, and usage questions. These signals were scattered and unstructured, making it hard to prioritize what to build next.

The core challenge: **understand our overseas users deeply enough to make product decisions that actually move the needle on retention and conversion**.

</En>

## 背景

Monica 是一款面向**海外用户**的 AI 浏览器插件，将 ChatGPT 级别的能力直接嵌入浏览器——Chat、快捷操作、阅读助手、ChatPDF、YouTube 摘要等。随着产品功能日益复杂，一个现象浮现：**大部分用户只用了皮毛**。他们用 Chat 和 Quick Action，忽略其他功能，也没有升级付费的意愿。

与此同时，用户反馈每天从邮箱涌入——功能请求、使用痛点、使用疑问。这些信号分散且无结构，很难判断优先级。

核心挑战：**深入理解海外用户，做出真正能提升留存和转化的产品决策**。

</Zh>

## What I Did

**Two tracks, one truth.** We ran two parallel research lines:

**Track 1: Daily email feedback.** Every day, I dug through user emails — feature requests, pain points, usage questions. These came from our most engaged overseas users, which gave us deep insights into power user behavior. A clear signal emerged: they kept mentioning **Quick Action**. But here's the catch — power users don't represent the majority. Their feedback is valuable but biased.

**Track 2: User surveys and interviews.** We ran 20,000-question surveys and 11 in-depth interviews with high-frequency users across different countries. This gave us the full picture: **almost all users only cared about a few features, and most didn't even know new features existed**. The gap between what users used and what the product offered was huge.

**The hypothesis.** Quick Action had the highest conversion rate of any feature. If we could surface more relevant features there, users would discover them naturally. But QA had its own problems — too many items overwhelm users, and irrelevant features create noise. So we did competitive analysis on how other browser plugins handle Quick Action design, then built our own solution.

**The insight from users.** The research surfaced clear patterns:

> *"I use Monica every day for work emails and document polishing. The sidebar is convenient — I don't need to open a new tab like ChatGPT."* — Unpaid user, UX Designer

> *"40 free queries are enough for me. I'm a student, I can't afford paid plans."* — Unpaid user, Student

> *"I subscribed for YouTube Summary. Before watching a video, I check the summary to see if it's worth my time."* — Paid user, AI Engineer, $240K+ income

> *"I used Monica for my master's thesis — reading 20-60 page papers with ChatPDF. But sometimes the PDF uploads but Monica can't read it."* — Paid user, Special Education Teacher

**Three user groups, each with different needs:**

- **Free users who don't explore (7/9)** — Used only Chat and Quick Actions, never discovered other features. Satisfied with basics, no motivation to upgrade.
- **Non-native English speakers (9/11)** — Used Monica primarily for email and document polishing — a workflow we hadn't optimized for.
- **Students needing academic tools (3/9)** — Needed to read papers, compare PDFs, preserve images during translation — features ChatPDF didn't handle well.

**What we optimized:** Quick Toolbar (surface high-value features), Language Translation (serve non-native speakers), PDF Reader ChatPDF (academic use cases).

**What we chose not to do.** Users asked for vibe coding, niche language support, and more. But based on cost, ROI, and engineering bandwidth, we deprioritized them. Saying no is as important as saying yes.

## 我的行动

**两条调研线，一个真相。** 我们同时推进两条调研：

**线路 1：每日邮箱反馈。** 每天定时挖掘有价值的邮箱反馈——功能请求、使用痛点、使用疑问。这些反馈来自最活跃的海外深度用户，能让我们洞察重度用户的行为。一个清晰的信号浮现：他们反复提到 **Quick Action** 的体验。但重度用户不代表大众——他们的反馈有价值，却有偏差。

**线路 2：用户调研。** 我们推进了 2 万份问卷和 11 位跨国高频用户的深度访谈，覆盖不同国家和地区。这给了我们完整的图景：**几乎所有用户只关心少部分功能，大部分用户发现有新功能却不知道也不会去了解**。用户使用的产品功能和产品实际提供的功能之间，存在巨大的鸿沟。

<div className="not-prose my-6">
<img src="/images/projects/monica-research-zh.svg" alt="调研流程" className="w-full rounded-xl bg-white/70" />

**假设：把更多功能放进 Quick Action。** 因为 QA 是转化率最高的入口。但 QA 也有问题——放太多功能会打扰用户，增加认知负担。所以我们做了竞品调研，看其他浏览器插件怎么设计 Quick Action，然后输出自己的方案。

**用户说了什么。** 调研揭示了清晰的用户画像：

> *"我每天用 Monica 处理工作邮件和润色文档。侧边栏很方便——不需要像 ChatGPT 那样开新标签页。"* — 免费用户，UX 设计师

> *"40 个免费 query 对我够用了。我是学生，买不起付费版。"* — 免费用户，学生

> *"我订阅是为了 YouTube Summary。看视频前先看摘要，判断值不值得花时间。"* — 付费用户，AI 工程师，收入 $240K+

> *"我用 Monica 写硕士论文——读 20-60 页的课程文档。但有时候 PDF 上传成功了 Monica 却读不出来。"* — 付费用户，特殊教育老师

**三类用户，每类需求不同：**

- **不探索功能的免费用户（7/9 受访者）** — 只用 Chat 和 Quick Actions，从未发现其他功能，满足于基础功能，没有升级动力
- **非英语母语用户（9/11 受访者）** — 主要用 Monica 润色邮件和文档——一个我们未专门优化的工作流
- **需要学术工具的学生（3/9 受访者）** — 需要读论文、多 PDF 对比、翻译保留图片——ChatPDF 之前做不好这些

**做了什么优化：** 快捷工具栏（前置高价值功能）、语言翻译（服务非母语用户）、PDF 阅读 ChatPDF（学术场景）。

## Impact

The research directly informed product decisions that moved the needle on feature adoption, retention, and churn.

**Feature discovery improved.** After optimizing the Quick Toolbar and language translation, users started exploring features they previously ignored. Feature penetration data shows the impact — Chat led at **11.22%**, followed by Reading (**8.31%**), Write (**7.57%**), and Search (**7.18%**). The Quick Toolbar redesign was the key driver, surfacing high-value features upfront.

**Retention went up, churn went down.** The combined optimizations led to measurable improvements:

- **Uninstall rate**: 36.47% → 18.1% (**-50%**)
- **Next-day retention**: 7.25% → 9.50% (**+31%**)

**Saying no was as important as saying yes.** Users requested many features (vibe coding, niche language support), but we deprioritized them based on cost, ROI, and engineering bandwidth. This disciplined approach kept the team focused on high-impact work.

## 影响

调研直接指导了产品决策，推动了功能发现、用户留存和流失率的改善。

**功能发现提升了。** 优化快捷工具栏和语言翻译后，用户开始探索之前忽略的功能。功能渗透率数据反映了效果——Chat 领先达到 **11.22%**，其次是 Reading（**8.31%**）、Write（**7.57%**）和 Search（**7.18%**）。快捷工具栏的重新设计是关键驱动力，将高价值功能前置展示。

**留存上升，流失下降。** 组合优化带来了可量化的改善：

- **卸载率**：36.47% → 18.1%（**-50%**）
- **次日留存**：7.25% → 9.50%（**+31%**）

**说不做和说做一样重要。** 用户提出了很多需求（vibe coding、小语种翻译），但我们基于成本、投入产出比和工程资源选择不优先推进。这种纪律性让团队聚焦在高影响力的工作上。
