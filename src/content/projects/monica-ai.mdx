## Background

Monica is an AI browser extension that brings ChatGPT-level capabilities directly into the browser — chat, quick actions, reading assistant, ChatPDF, YouTube summary, and more. As the product grew more complex, a pattern emerged: **most users only scratched the surface**. They used Chat and Quick Actions, ignored everything else, and had no reason to upgrade to paid plans.

The core question: **Why aren't users discovering the full value of the product, and how do we fix it?**

### The Insight

The problem wasn't feature quantity — it was **feature discoverability** and **value communication**. Users were satisfied with basic features, had no motivation to explore further, and didn't understand what they were missing. To fix this, we needed to understand users deeply before designing solutions.

</En>

## 背景

Monica 是一款 AI 浏览器插件，将 ChatGPT 级别的能力直接嵌入浏览器——Chat、快捷操作、阅读助手、ChatPDF、YouTube 摘要等。随着产品功能日益复杂，一个现象浮现：**大部分用户只用了皮毛**。他们用 Chat 和 Quick Action，忽略其他功能，也没有升级付费的意愿。

核心问题是：**用户为什么没有发现产品的全部价值，我们怎么解决？**

### 灵感

问题不在功能数量，而在**功能可发现性**和**价值传达**。用户满足于基础功能，没有探索动力，也不理解自己错过了什么。要解决这个问题，我们需要先深入理解用户，再设计解决方案。

## What I Did

### User Research

Led the full-cycle user research process:

- **Quantitative**: Analyzed 20,000 survey responses to identify user segments and behavior patterns
- **Qualitative**: Conducted 11 in-depth interviews (45 min each) with high-frequency US users — 2 paid, 9 free
- **Key insight**: Free users were "satisfied but shallow" — they used Chat (70-75% penetration) and Quick Actions (15-20%), but barely touched other features. Most were students who couldn't afford paid plans.

### User Segmentation & Strategy

Designed a **4-quadrant user classification** framework:

| | High Loyalty | Low Loyalty |
|---|---|---|
| **Paid** | Retain & deepen | Convert to loyal |
| **Free** | Convert to paid | Re-engage or churn |

This became the foundation for targeted product strategies — different tactics for different user groups.

### Feature Optimization

Based on research insights, designed and pushed forward three key optimizations:

- **Language Translation**: Enhanced for non-native English speakers (9/11 interviewees) who used Monica primarily for email and document polishing
- **Quick Toolbar**: Streamlined the most-used feature path, reducing friction for daily tasks
- **PDF Reader (ChatPDF)**: Optimized for academic use cases — students reading 20-60 page papers needed multi-PDF comparison and better translation that preserves images

## 我的行动

### 用户研究

主导了完整的用户研究流程：

- **定量分析**：分析 2 万份问卷数据，识别用户分群和行为模式
- **定性深访**：对 11 位高频美国用户进行深度访谈（每人 45 分钟），其中 2 位付费用户、9 位免费用户
- **核心洞察**：免费用户"满足但浅层"——Chat 渗透率 70-75%，Quick Action 15-20%，但几乎不触碰其他功能。大部分是学生党，没有付费能力。

### 用户分层与策略

设计了**四象限用户分类**框架：

| | 高忠诚度 | 低忠诚度 |
|---|---|---|
| **付费** | 留存 & 深挖 | 转化为忠诚 |
| **免费** | 转化为付费 | 重新激活或流失 |

这成为差异化产品策略的基础——不同用户群体用不同策略。

### 功能优化

基于调研洞察，主导设计并推进了三项关键优化：

- **语言翻译**：针对非英语母语用户（9/11 受访者）优化，他们主要用 Monica 润色邮件和文档
- **快捷工具栏**：精简最高频功能路径，降低日常任务的操作摩擦
- **PDF 阅读（ChatPDF）**：针对学术场景优化——学生需要阅读 20-60 页论文，需要多 PDF 对比和保留图片的翻译能力

</Zh>

## Innovation

**Data-Driven Product Decisions.** Every feature optimization was grounded in user research data, not assumptions. The 4-quadrant user classification framework became a reusable tool for prioritizing what to build, for whom, and why.

**Research-to-Action Pipeline.** Established a complete flow: survey data → deep interviews → insight extraction → feature prioritization → design → measurement. This pipeline can be replicated for any product optimization cycle.

**Cross-Cultural User Understanding.** Deep-dived into the needs of non-native English speakers in the US market — a segment often overlooked in AI tool design. The translation and PDF reader optimizations directly addressed their specific workflows.

</En>

<Zh>

## 创新性

**数据驱动的产品决策。** 每一项功能优化都基于用户调研数据而非假设。四象限用户分类框架成为可复用的工具，用于决定做什么、为谁做、为什么做。

**调研到行动的完整链路。** 建立了完整流程：问卷数据 → 深度访谈 → 洞察提取 → 功能优先级 → 设计 → 效果度量。这条链路可复制到任何产品优化周期。

**跨文化用户理解。** 深入研究了美国市场非英语母语用户的需求——这个在 AI 工具设计中常被忽视的群体。翻译和 PDF 阅读优化直接针对他们的工作流程。

</Zh>
